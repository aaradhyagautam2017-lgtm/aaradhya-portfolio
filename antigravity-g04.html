BIOGRAPHY
Design chose me before I chose it — I was drawing and making things long before I knew what a designer was. I studied Interaction Design at Delhi Technological University because I wanted a creative field with real constraints, where expression has to earn its place.
Empathy is how I start every problem. If I can’t feel what the user feels, I can’t design for them. I’m drawn to complex, uncomfortable problems — give me something big and ambiguous and I’ll work through it obsessively until the solution feels inevitable.
Outside of work: I cook, make 3D art, and explore concept art. Creativity doesn’t switch off.
